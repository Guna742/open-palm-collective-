Leadership Profile

Ananya Rao

Founder & Director

Ananya Rao is a social entrepreneur, cultural researcher, and public speaker with over 14 years of experience in community development, creative advocacy, and inclusive policy engagement.

Key Achievements

  • Member, National Council for Creative Economy
  • Guest speaker at University of Delhi, Tata Institute of Social Sciences, and regional leadership forums
  • Advisor to multiple grassroots art-based intervention programs

Publications

  • Art as Resistance (English)
  • Voices in Color (Tamil)
  • Contributor to journals on social innovation and cultural policy
