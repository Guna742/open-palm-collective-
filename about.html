Our Mission

Empowerment Through Art

We advance social justice by recognizing the rights of marginalized communities through education, creative expression, and inclusive economic initiatives.

Our work uses striking visuals and profound messages to revitalize public spaces and engage the community.

Our History

Founded in 2016

Established by Ananya Rao, our collective has grown from a small group of artists to a national movement.

Milestone: We were instrumental in the 2021 National Social Inclusion Framework policy dialogue.
